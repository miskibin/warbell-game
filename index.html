Hold the keep

Nightly ork sieges hammer the walls. Repair, fortify, and stand the line until dawn.

Swing, block, crit. Grunts, scouts, berserkers and warp-casting shamans, each with their own brain.

Forest, snow, desert, rock and swamp on one island — each with its own camp, wildlife and weather.

The horde pens captives like cattle. Clear a warband, open the cage, and they fight at your side.
