What's inside

A living warband sim

Not just a defense game — a whole island that works, fights and grows around you.

⚔️

Real-time combat

Swing, crit and block with stamina. Four ork breeds — grunts, scouts, berserkers and shamans — each with their own brain and bite.

🏰

City-building

Lay out plots and raise houses, walls, towers and workshops. A real town economy with food, repair and burn.

🪓

Villagers that work

Woodcutters fell real trees. Miners chip real boulders and cart the stone home. Recruit them, feed them, grow the town.

🌳

The War Table

A branching upgrade tree — blade, armor, abilities and defenses. Spend gold and stone to climb it night after night.

🗺️

Five biomes

Forest, snow, desert, rock and swamp on one enlarged island — each with its own ork camp, wildlife, weather and loot.

🛡️

Defenses & the bell

Archer towers, ballistae and shrines you unlock and place. Ring the war bell to call the night on your own terms.

🔓

Free the caged

The horde pens captives like cattle. Clear a warband, break the cage open, and they take up arms at your side.

💀

Gnashfang Hold

An off-map ork fortress beyond the swamp feeds the waves — patrols, the Blight, and towers that fire on sight.

The Bloodline

You are not one knight.
You are a line — and the blade does not fall when you do.

When the hero falls, the next heir takes up the sword with every upgrade intact. Your townsfolk are your lives. Lose the keep, or lose the last of the bloodline — only then is it over.
